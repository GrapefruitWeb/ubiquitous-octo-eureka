    Find out what I can do for you and your home

    Free Preliminary Consultation

    If you know that your home isn't working for you but not sure where to start, I can meet with you at your property and discuss your ideas, requirements and budget. With  excellent knowledge of what's possible both structurally and how to create the perfect flow of spaces, I can help you achieve your

    goals.


    From this I can create an outline brief for the project and produce a written quotation for the project for the various stages below including the local council fees and charges for consultants such as structural engineers if/where required.


    If you are buying a new property and want to know what changes are possible to make, I’d be delighted to visit the property with you and discuss the project.

    Stage 1 - Architectural Survey

    Once appointed, I will arrange to undertake a measured survey of the building which will allow me to prepare a detailed set of existing drawings that I will use as a starting point for your project.

    Stage 2 – Initial Design

    From these drawings and the project brief we have discussed, we will work with you to develop a scheme which works for you both aesthetically and financially. We will produce sketches and a 3D model, to help you visualise the possibilities of the scheme.

    Stage 3 - Planning Permission

    If the project requires Planning Permission, I will submit drawings and application forms to the local authority and resolve any issues with the Planning Officer. In some cases, Planning Permission is not required and I will communicate with the Council to obtain written confirmation that it is a 'Permitted Development'. The council has 2 months to make a decision from the date the application has been validated.


    Stage 4 - Building Warrant

    At this stage, I will prepare the drawings to show materials, construction method, electrical and drainage layouts, kitchen and bathroom design, specifications and U-value calculations for insulation. If required, I can arrange for an engineer to produce drawings, specifications and certification for all the new structural elements.


    Once all this information has been submitted and acknowledged, the Council has 20 days to respond with points they are not satisfied with or requesting more information. This process can take around 6-8 weeks.


    Stage 5 - Tender

    Depending on the scale and budget of your project, if required I can help you by producing a tender package so that you can get a number of builders to supply price quotes for your project.

    Project Management

    Again, if required, I can manage the project on site. This would be charged at an hourly rate, if only occasional visits on site are required, or a fixed fee can be agreed if the project is seen to require weekly visits, meetings and administration of the Building Contract.


    The service is based on the current RIAS Architects Appointment.


    I carry the appropriate Professional Indemnity Insurance.
