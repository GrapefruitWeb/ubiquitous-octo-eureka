    Services

    Services

    Are you just looking for some advice on how you could enhance your property or you have firm thoughts of what you want, I’m more than happy to talk through your design ideas with you. We can come up with a design that you agree is exactly what you want. I can help to guide you through the council approvals that you’ll need for planning permission and building warrant, whilst looking after all drawings, reports and application forms needed to gain the permissions.

    House Extensions

    Whether you are just looking for a practical way to generate more rooms in your home or wish to

    create bigger and better spaces, I’m highly experienced in designing house extension projects

    regardless of size or budget.

    From small additions to a substantial transformation, I can guide you through the process from

    inception to completion.

    Internal Alterations

    If you’re looking at your home and wishing you had more space, sometimes all it takes is a little

    reconfiguration to produce your ideal layout. Removing walls can create open-plan living, or dividing

    up rooms to gain that office you need. Simply by bringing in more natural daylight or having more

    access to the garden can make the all difference.

    Attic Conversions

    Opening up an existing roof space can be a great and cost-effective way of creating additional space

    within your home. I can help identify whether your attic can easily be converted and creatively advise

    on potential layouts, ideal stair locations and the potential use of rooflights and dormer windows.

    Garage Conversions

    Is your garage being used to keep things in other than parking the car? This additional space could be

    suitable to create cost-effective habitable accommodation as an extension to your home. I can advise you on design issues to ensure your garage conversion integrates itself perfectly with your existing

    home.
